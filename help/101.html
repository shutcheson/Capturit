Getting Started

Welcome to Capturit! To begin using our app, please follow the steps below to create your Capturit account.

Creating an Account

  • 1. Download the Capturit app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  • 2. Install and launch the Capturit app.
  • 3. Tap on ‘Sign up now’ at the bottom of the screen to create your account.
  • 4. Enter your first and last name, followed by your email, then click ‘Next’.
  • 5. Click ‘Change’ to select your profile picture, then click ‘Next’.
  • 6. Enter a unique personal username. Remember to choose wisely. This cannot be changed for 60 days.
  • 7. Set and confirm your password. Ensure it is at least 12 characters long, including a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • 8. Click ‘Verify account’. A verification email will be sent to your email address. Click the verification link to complete the process.
  • 9. You will then be taken back to your new Capturit profile.

If you need further assistance, our support team is always here to help.
