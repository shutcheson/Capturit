Help & Support

FAQs

Find answers to common questions about using Capturit. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please Contact Support.

General Questions

  • Download the Capturit app from the App Store or Google Play Store, then follow the in-app instructions to sign up and verify your account.

  • Yes, Capturit is free to download and use. However, certain premium features may be available for purchase.

  • Go to the login screen, click ‘Forgot Password’, and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

Account Management

  • Navigate to ‘Account Settings’ within the app, select ‘Email’, and follow the prompts to update your email address.

  • Usernames can be changed once every 60 days. Go to ‘Account Settings’, select ‘Username’, and enter a new, unique username.

  • Please refer to our Deleting Account Data page for detailed instructions on how to permanently delete your account and data.

Privacy and Security

  • Capturit employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data. For more details, visit our Security Measures page.

  • Navigate to ‘Privacy Settings’ within the app to control who can see your content and what information is shared.

  • All your personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers. Please review our Data Retention Policy for more information.

Using the Capturit App

  • Go to the ‘Upload’ section in the app, select the media you wish to upload, and follow the prompts to complete the process.

  • Select the content you want to share, click the ‘Share’ button, and choose the platform or contacts you want to share with.

  • Navigate to the ‘Events’ section in the app to create, edit, and manage your events, and invite others to join.
